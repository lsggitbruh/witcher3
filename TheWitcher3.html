The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - Review

Welcome to my gaming review site, focusing on the best RPGs and shooters!

Why The Witcher 3 Hits Different

Exploration Freedom: The Witcher 3 gives you incredible freedom to travel, but every location has its own random encounters that actually enhance the experience.

Meaningful Combat: The world is filled with monsters of considerable challenge, but they don't feel random - they enhance the story and make every encounter matter.

Respects the Source: Even with some discrepancies from the books, the game never distracts from Sapkowski's original masterpiece. As someone reading the books, I can appreciate how they complement each other.

My Rating

This is what RPGs should aspire to be. Essential gaming.
